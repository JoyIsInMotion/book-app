Dune

by Frank Herbert

Science Fiction

Set in the distant future amidst a feudal interstellar society, Dune tells the story of young Paul Atreides, whose family accepts the stewardship of the desert planet Arrakis. As the only producer of the most valuable substance in the universe — the spice melange — Arrakis is a world of immense political intrigue and danger.
